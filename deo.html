Welcome to Deoghar : Spiritual city

Baidyanath Temple

If you ask me, what Deoghar is famous for? I’d say the devotion of millions of devotees for Lord Shiva which makes them visit the Baidyanath Dham often.


-Alongside, it is the most sacred place in Jharkhand and an ancient temple of Baba Baidyanath.


-It is also referred to as Deoghar, believed to be the place where Ravana had carried Lord Shiva on his way to Sri Lanka though he failed to do that due to violation of its non-stop-journey.


-If you love to discover spirituality, culture, and nature, pack your bags and get ready to go.


Nualakha Temple

-The best time to visit Deoghar is a subjective topic as the movement you enter this religious place becomes your best time.


-If intricate design and craftsmanship are the things your eyes are craving to spot.


-The temple is entirely dedicated to the deities of Hindus Radha and Krishna which leads to an environment for meditation and reflection.


-Then, you must consider the impressive architecture of Naulakha Temple, which is also considered to be reminiscent of the famous Ramakrishna Mission in Belur, West Bengal.


Satsang Ashram

It is one of the renowned places in Deoghar and a center of many cultural and religious events also including satsangs.

-Such types of events have a huge crowd that shares devotion and community spirit.


-You must go to such an Ashram if you are looking for a sense of meaning, purpose, and connection with other people


Trikuta Hills

It is a Hindu pilgrimage site and the holy shrine of Vaishno Devi that is 15 km away from the city of Deoghar.


-If you are always ready for an adventure then this holy place can give you the pleasure and excitement you may be missing in life.


-The place has very great mythological significance that is associated with the epic Ramayana.


-It is believed that Lord Hanuman had picked Sanjeevani herb from these hills to save Lakchaman’s life.


-If you find meditation the only way to peace of mind, then this hill is going to transform your meditation journey with its spiritual energy..
