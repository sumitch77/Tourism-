Welcome to Netarhat : The Queen of Chotanagpur

Netarhat Sunrise

A beautiful sunrise view from Netarhat, known for its scenic beauty and tranquility.


-Netarhat offers stunning sunrise views from Magnolia Point.


-Misty hills glow as the sun rises.


-Early mornings draw many visitors.


-A perfect spot for nature and photography lovers.


Netarhat Waterfall

-A stunning waterfall in Netarhat, surrounded by lush greenery and perfect for nature lovers.


-Netarhat boasts scenic waterfalls set in lush forests and hills.


-Lower Ghaghri Falls plunges nearly 100 meters in a wild, green setting.


-Upper Ghaghri Falls offers calm views just 4 km from town.


-Lodh Falls, 60 km away, is Jharkhand’s tallest and most powerful cascade.

Netarhat Hill Station

A serene hill station in Jharkhand, offering breathtaking views and a peaceful environment.

-Netarhat is a serene hill station in the Latehar district of Jharkhand, often referred to as the "Queen of Chotanagpur."


=It is situated at an altitude of around 1,100 meters and is known for its pleasant climate throughoutthe year.


-The region is famous for stunning views of sunrise and sunset, especially from Magnolia Point.


-Surrounded by dense forests and waterfalls, it offers a perfect escape for nature lovers.


Netarhat Grounds

A mesmerizing sunset view from Netarhat, perfect for ending your day with tranquility.


-Netarhat’s grounds are covered with lush green meadows and dense forests.


-The area is perfect for walking, picnics, and nature exploration.


-Soft grasslands and open spaces offer peaceful retreats.


-The grounds are rich in flora and often visited by birdwatchers.
