Betla National Park

Betla National Park is a wildlife sanctuary located in Latehar district of Jharkhand, India.


It is known for its diverse flora and fauna, including tigers, leopards, and elephants.


The park is a popular destination for jungle safaris and nature enthusiasts.


Betla National Park offers a unique opportunity to explore the natural beauty and wildlife of Jharkhand.
