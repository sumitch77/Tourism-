Welcome to Hazaribagh National Park : A Heaven for Wildlife

Wildlife Safari

Explore the dense forests and grasslands of Hazaribagh on an adventurous safari.


-Spread across 184 sq. km of protected land.


-Home to tigers, leopards, nilgai, sambhar, and wild boars.


-Perfect for eco-tourism and adventure seekers.


-Guided safaris provide a thrilling experience of the wild.


Birdwatching

A paradise for bird lovers, with countless species fluttering in the lush greenery.


-Migratory birds visit the park during winter months.


-Spot peacocks, parakeets, kingfishers, and eagles.


-Dense forests provide safe habitats for rare species.


-Perfect for photographers and nature enthusiasts.


Scenic Landscapes

Rolling hills, green valleys, and peaceful streams enhance the charm of the park.


-Forests are filled with sal, bamboo, and tropical trees.


-Sunlight filters beautifully through the canopy.


-Great for trekking, hiking, and eco-camping.


-A peaceful escape into untouched wilderness.


Eco-Tourism

Hazaribagh National Park promotes eco-tourism and nature conservation.


-Nature walks and guided tours are available.


-Camping options for adventure enthusiasts.


-Ideal for school trips, researchers, and families.


-A blend of learning, adventure, and relaxation.
