Welcome to Parasnath : The Sacred Peak of Jharkhand

Parasnath Hill

The highest peak in Jharkhand and a sacred site for Jains, offering breathtaking panoramic views.


-Also known as Shikharji, rising 1,365 meters above sea level.


-Considered the most important pilgrimage site for Jain devotees.


-Surrounded by lush forests and natural beauty.


-Offers trekking trails leading to the summit and temples.


Parasnath Jain Temples

A sacred complex of temples atop Parasnath Hill, dedicated to Jain Tirthankaras.


-23 of the 24 Jain Tirthankaras are believed to have attained salvation here.


-Temples are known for their serene architecture and religious importance.


-Pilgrims undertake long treks barefoot to reach the temples.


-One of the most visited spiritual sites in Jharkhand.


Parasnath Trek

A thrilling trekking route that leads devotees and tourists to the sacred peak.


-Trek length: around 9 km from Madhuban to the summit.


-Path winds through forests, streams, and scenic landscapes.


-Popular among both pilgrims and adventure seekers.


-Best experienced during cool winter months for comfort.


Parasnath Forests

The dense green forests surrounding Parasnath Hill, home to diverse flora and fauna.


-Forests enhance the spiritual and natural beauty of the hill.


-Birdwatchers and nature lovers frequent these areas.


-Rich biodiversity with medicinal plants and wildlife.


-A peaceful retreat for meditation and exploration.
