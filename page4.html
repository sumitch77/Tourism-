The Hundru Falls is created on the course of the Subarnarekha River.


- where it falls from a height of 98 metres (322 ft) creating one of the highest water falls in the state.


- It is 45 kilometres (28 mi) from Ranchi.


-A perfect spot for nature and photography lovers.


Rock Garden

-Rock Garden in Jharkhand is a popular tourist spot located on Gonda Hill in Ranchi.


- adorned with natural rocks.


- sculptures and waterfalls and is a great place for a picnic.


- It is about 4 kilometres from the city centre.


- can be seen from Kanke Dam.

Kanke Dam

Kanke Dam (Kanke Dam) is a dam located in Ranchi.


-the capital of Jharkhand.


-built by the British and providing drinking water to a large part of the city.


-It is also a popular spot for boating, fishing and bathing.


- Supplying drinking water to about 30% of Ranchi's area.
