Patratu Lake

-A beautiful view from Patratu Lake Resort, known for its scenic beauty and tranquility.

-The main lake associated with Patratu Valley is Patratu Lake

-Which is a large reservoir formed by the Patratu Dam on the Nalkarni river and is crucial for the Patratu Thermal Power Station.

-While the valley is known for its natural beauty and dense forests, Patratu Lake is the prominent water body, offering activities like motorboat rides and a resort, and is a central feature of the region's tourism

Water Parks

-Water Slides: Towering and twisting slides of various heights and speeds for thrill-seekers.

-Lazy Rivers: Gently flowing rivers where visitors can relax on tubes, drifting peacefully along.

-Splash Pads & Spraygrounds: Interactive water play areas designed for children with sprayers and water jets.

-Swimming Pools: Areas for swimming and various other forms of aquatic recreation.

-Bathing & Barefooting: Environments for general bathing and enjoying water in other ways.

Boating

-Boating is a popular tourist activity in Jharkhand, especially at the numerous dams and lakes in the state, which offer scenic views and recreational opportunities.


-Key locations for boating include Chandil Dam for high-speed thrill rides.


-Ladhna Dam for serene wooden boat rides surrounded by mountains.


-Patratu Dam for its water supply and scenic beauty, and Dimna Lake which is a wellknown spot in the East Singhbhum district.
