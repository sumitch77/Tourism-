Welcome to Dassam Falls : The Cascading Beauty of Ranchi

Main Waterfall View

Dassam Falls is one of the most popular waterfalls near Ranchi, known for its stunning beauty.


-Located on the Kanchi River, about 40 km from Ranchi.


-Water plunges from a height of around 144 feet (44 meters).


-The fall creates a thunderous sound as it crashes into the pool below.


-A must-visit for nature lovers and photographers.


Surrounding Landscapes

The area around Dassam Falls is filled with lush greenery and rocky terrains.


-Thick forests add to the natural charm of the place.


-A great picnic spot for families and friends.


-During monsoon, the flow becomes more powerful and breathtaking.


-Offers an ideal escape from city life.


Trekking & Adventure

Dassam Falls is not just scenic but also adventurous for thrill seekers.


-Steep steps lead down to the waterfall viewpoint.


-Trekking through nearby forest trails adds excitement.


-The rocks and slopes make it a favorite for adventure lovers.


-Best visited during winter and post-monsoon season.


Tourist Experience

A popular tourist destination in Jharkhand, attracting visitors throughout the year.


-Visitors enjoy breathtaking views from multiple points.


-Photography opportunities at every corner.


-Food stalls and resting areas nearby for tourists.


-A peaceful retreat close to Ranchi city.
