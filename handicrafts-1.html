Tribal Handicrafts

Explore the rich heritage of Jharkhand through bamboo art, dokra brass work, wooden carvings, pottery, and the ancient Paitkar paintings.Each piece a story crafted by skilled tribal artisans.

Featured Handicrafts

🌿 Bamboo Art

Crafted by skilled artisans, bamboo baskets, mats, and home décor items reflect sustainable living and eco-friendly traditions of Jharkhand.

✨ Dokra Brass Work

Intricately carved idols, masks, and cultural artifacts showcasing the deep artistic heritage of Jharkhand’s tribal communities.

🪵 Wooden Carvings

Beautifully carved masks, idols, and panels made from local wood. These carvings carry spiritual and cultural significance, blending fine artistry with the essence of Jharkhand’s folklore.

🏺 Tribal Pottery

Elegant clay pots, cups, and décor painted with bright tribal designs. These eco-friendly creations bring warmth and authenticity, perfect for traditional kitchens and modern homes alike.

🎨 Paitkar Painting

Paitkar paintings, also known as scroll paintings, are among the oldest tribal art forms of India. Traditionally created with natural colors from clay, charcoal, and leaves, these artworks depict folk tales, rituals, and community life. Once painted on walls with Miswak sticks, today they continue to flourish on scrolls and canvases, keeping centuries of storytelling alive.
